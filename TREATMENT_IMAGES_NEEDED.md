# Treatment Pages - Images Required

This document lists all the newly enhanced treatment pages and the images needed for each page, following the existing naming pattern.

## Image Naming Pattern
- Base URL: `https://ik.imagekit.io/jaishreeskinfinitii/websiteimages/{treatment-folder}/`
- Hero images: `{treatment-name}.webp`
- Treatment areas: `treatment-areas/{area-name}.webp` (lowercase, spaces to hyphens, special chars removed)
- Treatment benefits: `treatment-benefits/{benefit-name}.webp` (lowercase, spaces to hyphens)
- Treatment indications: `treatment-indications/{indication-name}.webp` (lowercase, spaces to hyphens)

## Notes

1. All images should be in `.webp` format for optimal performance
2. Image names follow the pattern: lowercase, spaces replaced with hyphens, special characters removed
3. Treatment areas/benefits/indications are converted to lowercase with hyphens
4. Special characters like `&` are replaced with `and`, parentheses are removed
5. Accented characters (like `é` in décolletage) should be kept as-is or converted to standard characters based on your preference
